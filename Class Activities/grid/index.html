About ENHYPEN

ENHYPEN is a seven-member K-pop boy group formed by Belift Lab, a joint venture between CJ ENM and Big Hit Entertainment. The group was formed through the 2020 survival competition show "I-LAND" and debuted on November 30, 2020, with their extended play (EP) titled Border: Day One.

ENHYPEN's name symbolizes connection, discovery, and growth. The group connects people, just like how a hyphen connects different words to create new meanings. Since their debut, ENHYPEN has gained massive popularity both domestically and internationally, known for their synchronized choreography, powerful music, and unique concepts.

Achievements

ENHYPEN has won numerous awards, including:

  • Best New Artist at the Mnet Asian Music Awards (2020)
  • Rookie of the Year at the Golden Disc Awards (2021)
  • Top 10 Artists at the Melon Music Awards (2021)
